Dermatology FAQ

Common questions about skin treatments at our Mangaluru clinic. Expert answers from our certified skin specialists.

What is the best treatment for acne?

The best treatment for acne depends on your skin type and the severity of breakouts. At Skin 360, we combine medical-grade topical therapies with clinical procedures like chemical peels and laser therapy for optimal results.

How long does laser skin treatment take?

Most laser skin treatment sessions take between 30 to 60 minutes depending on the area being treated. Significant results are typically visible after 3-6 sessions, spaced a few weeks apart.

Is hair loss treatment permanent?

While many hair loss treatments like PRP therapy and medical management can significantly restore hair density, long-term results often require a consistent maintenance plan tailored by your hair specialist.

Is pigmentation treatment safe?

Yes, pigmentation treatments at our clinic are highly safe when performed by a certified dermatologist. We use evidence-based methods and medical-grade equipment to ensure safe and effective dark spot removal.
