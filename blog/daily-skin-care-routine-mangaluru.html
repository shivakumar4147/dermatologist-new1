Daily Skin Care Routine Recommended by Dermatologists

By Dr. Sarah Wilson | March 8, 2024 | 10 min read

A consistent daily skincare routine is the secret to long-term skin health and radiance. However, with thousands of products on the market, it's easy to get overwhelmed. At our dermatology clinic in Mangaluru, we advocate for a "less is more" approach that focuses on high-quality medical essentials. In this guide, we break down the ideal routine for healthy skin.

1. Morning: Protect and Prevent

The goal of your morning routine should be to protect your skin from environmental damage, including UV rays and pollution.

  • Cleansing: Use a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser to remove any oil or sweat accumulated overnight.
  • Antioxidant Serum: Apply a Vitamin C serum to neutralize free radicals and brighten your complexion.
  • Moisturization: Use a lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizer to maintain the skin barrier.
  • Sunscreen: The most important step. Use a broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 50.

2. Evening: Repair and Restore

Your nighttime routine is when the real work happens. This is the time to use active ingredients that repair the skin while you sleep.

  • Double Cleanse: Use an oil-based cleanser followed by your regular cleanser to ensure all makeup and sunscreen are removed.
  • Targeted Treatment: This is when you apply retinoids, peptides, or specific treatments for acne or pigmentation.
  • Night Cream: Use a slightly richer moisturizer to support the skin's natural repair process overnight.

3. Weekly Extras

Once or twice a week, you can add an exfoliating treatment (like a gentle chemical exfoliant) to remove dead skin cells and keep your pores clear. Avoid over-exfoliating, as this can lead to redness and sensitivity.
