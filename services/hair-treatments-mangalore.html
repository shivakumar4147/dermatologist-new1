Personalized Hair Care

Hair concerns are multifactorial—genetics, hormones, nutrition, scalp health, stress, and hair-care practices all play roles. Skin 360 approaches hair treatment with careful diagnosis and stepwise therapy to protect scalp integrity while improving density, reducing shedding, and controlling flaking.

Hair Loss Treatment

Management depends on type and stage: androgenetic hair loss, telogen effluvium, or scarring alopecias. Plans may include topical minoxidil, peptide serums, oral therapies, nutritional support, and clinic procedures such as PRP when indicated. We pair treatments with realistic timelines and maintenance strategies to sustain gains. Learn more about hair loss care.

Dandruff Treatment

Dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis respond to antifungal shampoos, anti-inflammatory agents, and supportive routines. We advise correct frequency, contact time, and regimen adjustments to keep the scalp comfortable and flake-free without excessive dryness. Explore dandruff treatment.

Scalp Treatment

We address scalp sensitivity, oil balance, and buildup with tailored cleansers, exfoliants, and soothing care. For specific scalp conditions, targeted medications and clinic procedures restore comfort and health. Read about scalp care.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will shedding reduce? Many patients notice reduced shedding within 8–12 weeks, with density improvements over several months depending on diagnosis and plan adherence.

Is PRP necessary? Not for everyone. We discuss candidacy, expectations, and alternatives. The foundation remains medical therapy and scalp health.

Will dandruff return? Maintenance routines keep flaking controlled. We provide clear guidance on shampoo cycles and supportive care to sustain comfort.

Are treatments safe? Yes—protocols are selected for your needs and monitored by a dermatologist with safety-first settings and sequenced intensity.
