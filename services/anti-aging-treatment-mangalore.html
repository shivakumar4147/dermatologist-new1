Understanding the Aging Process

As we age, the production of essential structural proteins like collagen and elastin slows down. Additionally, the skin's hyaluronic acid content decreases, leading to a loss of volume and hydration. Our skin specialist in Mangaluru categorizes aging into two main types:

  • Intrinsic Aging: The inevitable chronological aging process that affects everyone over time.
  • Extrinsic Aging: Also known as "photo-aging," this is caused by environmental factors, primarily UV radiation, which accelerates the breakdown of collagen and leads to premature wrinkles and spots.

Patient FAQs: Anti-Aging in Mangaluru

When should I start anti-aging treatments?

While there is no fixed age, starting in your late 20s or early 30s can help maintain collagen levels and prevent deep wrinkles from forming. It's much easier to prevent aging than to reverse it.

Are anti-aging treatments permanent?

While treatments like MNRF and Laser provide long-lasting structural improvements, the aging process continues. Periodic maintenance sessions are recommended to sustain the youthful effects.

Is there any downtime after the procedures?

Most of our rejuvenation treatments have minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness for 24-48 hours, but you can typically return to your normal routine almost immediately.

Will I look "fake" after the treatment?

Absolutely not. At Skin 360, we specialize in "subtle enhancements." Our techniques are designed to enhance your natural features and restore what has been lost over time.

Recovery and Results

Post-treatment recovery varies depending on the intensity of the procedure. For non-invasive treatments like skin polishing or light peels, there is zero downtime. For more intensive therapies like MNRF, you might experience mild redness and swelling for a day or two. We provide comprehensive aftercare kits and detailed instructions to ensure your skin heals optimally and your results are maximized.
