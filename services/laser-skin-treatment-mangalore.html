Patient FAQs: Laser Skin Treatment in Mangaluru

Is laser treatment painful?

Most modern lasers feel like a slight "rubber band snap" on the skin. Our integrated cooling systems and topical numbing creams make the procedure very tolerable for most patients.

How many sessions of laser hair removal do I need?

Typically, 6-8 sessions are required for significant and long-lasting hair reduction, as the laser can only target hair in its active growth phase.

Are there any side effects?

Temporary redness or mild swelling is common for a few hours. When performed by experts using the right settings, serious side effects like burns or scarring are extremely rare.

Can I go out in the sun after laser?

Direct sun exposure should be avoided for at least 48-72 hours post-laser. Use of a broad-spectrum sunscreen is mandatory to protect the treated area from pigmentation.

Recovery and Results

Most laser procedures involve minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness or a mild sunburnt sensation for a few hours to a day. We provide comprehensive post-laser care instructions, including the mandatory use of high-SPF sunscreen and specialized moisturizers to protect your treated skin. Visible results often appear after the first few sessions, with cumulative improvements over a complete treatment course.
