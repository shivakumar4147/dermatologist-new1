Brightening with Care

Pigmentation needs patient, methodical care with respect for the skin barrier. At Skin 360, we evaluate the type and distribution of pigmentation, contributing factors, and current routines to design safe brightening programs. Core actives may include azelaic acid, kojic acid, arbutin, niacinamide, and gentle exfoliation, combined with daily broad-spectrum sunscreen and supportive moisturization.

Melasma & PIH

Melasma requires strict sun protection and slow, steady progress—overly aggressive peels can worsen outcomes. Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation responds to anti-inflammatory care, gentle exfoliation, and targeted lightening agents. Device-based therapies are considered when appropriate to skin type and stability.

Clinic Procedures

For selected cases, chemical peels and light devices can complement topical care. We prioritize low-irritation protocols and staged intensity to prevent rebound or sensitivity. Maintenance is essential to preserve results and reduce recurrence.
