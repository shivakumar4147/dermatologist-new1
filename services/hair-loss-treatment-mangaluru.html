Common Causes of Hair Loss and Thinning

Understanding why your hair is falling out is the first step toward a successful treatment. Our dermatologist in Mangaluru evaluates several possible triggers:

  • Androgenetic Alopecia (Pattern Baldness): The most common cause, primarily driven by genetics and hormonal changes (DHT). It affects both men (receding hairline, crown thinning) and women (widening part, overall thinning).
  • Telogen Effluvium: A temporary form of hair loss often triggered by physical or emotional stress, severe illness, surgery, or rapid weight loss. This causes hair to shift prematurely into the shedding phase.
  • Alopecia Areata: An autoimmune condition where the body's immune system attacks hair follicles, leading to smooth, round bald patches on the scalp or beard.
  • Scalp Conditions: Chronic dandruff (seborrheic dermatitis), fungal infections, or psoriasis can inflame the scalp and weaken hair roots.
  • Nutritional Deficiencies: Lack of essential vitamins and minerals like iron, zinc, biotin, and protein can significantly impact hair growth cycles.

Advanced Hair Regrowth Treatments at Skin 360

We provide a comprehensive suite of treatments designed to stimulate follicles and nourish the scalp:

  • Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy: A highly effective, non-surgical procedure where growth factors from your own blood are concentrated and injected into the scalp to revitalize dormant follicles and increase hair density.
  • Medical Management: Prescription-strength topical solutions (like Minoxidil), peptide serums, and oral medications (like Finasteride or Dutasteride) tailored to your specific diagnosis and health profile.
  • Mesotherapy: A technique where a cocktail of vitamins, minerals, and growth factors is micro-injected into the scalp to improve blood circulation and nutrient delivery to the roots.
  • Nutritional Counseling: Customized supplement plans to address internal deficiencies that may be hindering your hair's growth potential.
  • Scalp Detox and Rejuvenation: Specialized clinical treatments to clear scalp buildup, reduce inflammation, and create the ideal environment for healthy hair growth.

When to See a Hair Specialist?

Early intervention is critical in hair loss management. You should consider a consultation at our dermatology clinic in Mangaluru if you notice:

  • Increased Shedding: Noticing significantly more hair than usual on your pillow, in the shower drain, or on your hairbrush.
  • Visible Thinning: If your scalp is becoming more visible, particularly at the crown or along your parting line.
  • Receding Hairline: A gradual movement of the hairline backward from the forehead.
  • Bald Patches: Sudden appearance of smooth, hairless spots on the scalp or facial hair.
  • Scalp Irritation: Persistent itching, burning, or redness accompanied by hair fall.
