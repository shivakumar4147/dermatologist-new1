Expert Hair Loss Solutions in Mangaluru

Hair loss can be a stressful experience, affecting both men and women. At Skin 360, we offer a comprehensive approach to hair loss treatment in Mangaluru. Our hair specialist evaluates your history, pattern of loss, and possible triggers to define a plan specific to your diagnosis.

Common Causes of Hair Loss

Understanding the root cause is the first step toward effective treatment. Our dermatologist in Mangaluru identifies these common factors:

  • Androgenetic Alopecia: Also known as male or female pattern baldness, which is primarily genetic and hormonal.
  • Telogen Effluvium: Temporary hair shedding often triggered by stress, illness, or nutritional deficiencies.
  • Alopecia Areata: An autoimmune condition causing patchy hair loss.
  • Scalp Infections: Fungal or bacterial infections that can damage hair follicles.
  • Lifestyle Factors: Poor diet, harsh hair treatments, and lack of proper scalp care.

Advanced Treatment Options

We provide a variety of medical and procedural treatments to promote hair regrowth and maintain scalp health:

  • PRP Therapy (Platelet-Rich Plasma): Using your own blood's growth factors to stimulate dormant hair follicles.
  • Medical Management: Prescription-strength topical minoxidil, peptide serums, and oral medications when appropriate.
  • Nutritional Support: Targeted supplements to address deficiencies that impact hair growth.
  • Scalp Treatments: Specialized care for dandruff, seborrheic dermatitis, and other scalp conditions.

When to See a Hair Specialist?

Early intervention is key to preserving hair density. You should consider a consultation at our dermatology clinic in Mangaluru if you notice:

  • Sudden or excessive hair shedding (clumps in the drain or on your brush).
  • Visible thinning at the crown or a receding hairline.
  • Patchy bald spots appearing on the scalp or beard.
  • Persistent itching, flaking, or redness on the scalp.

Why Choose Skin 360 for Hair Care?

Consulting the best hair specialist in Mangaluru ensures that your treatment is based on a medical diagnosis rather than trial and error with cosmetic products. We focus on consistency, realistic expectations, and long-term maintenance regimens to support stable results.
