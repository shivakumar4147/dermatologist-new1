When to See a Skin Specialist?

Many people wait until a condition becomes severe before seeking help. However, early intervention by a dermatologist in mangalore can prevent complications and lead to faster recovery. You should consider a consultation if you experience:

  • Persistent Acne: Breakouts that don't respond to over-the-counter treatments.
  • Unusual Moles: Changes in size, shape, or color of existing moles.
  • Chronic Rashes: Eczema, psoriasis, or unexplained skin irritation.
  • Hair and Scalp Issues: Excessive hair fall, thinning, or persistent dandruff.
  • Skin Discoloration: Sudden dark spots, melasma, or uneven skin tone.

Our Consultation Process

A standard consultation at our dermatology clinic in mangalore involves:

  • Medical History Review: Understanding your health background and previous treatments.
  • Physical Examination: A detailed look at the affected skin area under professional lighting.
  • Diagnosis: Identifying the specific condition and its underlying causes.
  • Treatment Planning: Discussing medication, procedures, and lifestyle adjustments.
