Understanding Acne: Causes and Symptoms

Acne is primarily caused by excess oil production, clogged hair follicles, bacteria, and inflammation. Common symptoms include:

  • Whiteheads and Blackheads: Non-inflammatory clogged pores.
  • Papules and Pustules: Red, inflamed bumps often filled with pus.
  • Nodules and Cysts: Deep, painful lumps that carry a high risk of scarring.

Advanced Treatment Methods

Our dermatology clinic in Mangaluru offers a range of medical-grade treatments:

  • Topical & Oral Medications: Retinoids, antibiotics, and hormonal therapy.
  • Chemical Peels: Salicylic or mandelic acid peels to unclog pores.
  • Comedone Extraction: Safe removal of blackheads and whiteheads.
  • Laser & Light Therapy: To target bacteria and reduce inflammation.

Expected Results and Recovery

Most patients see a significant reduction in active acne within 4-8 weeks. Clinical procedures like peels may involve minor redness for 1-2 days, but there is typically no downtime. Consistent follow-ups at our Mangaluru skin clinic are essential for long-term maintenance.
