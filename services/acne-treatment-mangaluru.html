Clear Skin with Evidence-Based Acne Care

At Skin 360, acne treatment begins with a thorough dermatology consultation to understand your skin type, breakout pattern, triggers, and previous therapies. Our dermatologist designs a personalized plan that balances effectiveness and tolerance, focusing on both active acne control and long-term skin health.

Acne presents in several forms—whiteheads, blackheads, papules, pustules, nodules, and cysts—and may be influenced by hormones, stress, diet, cosmetics, and genetics. Effective treatment requires a structured approach with appropriate topical and oral medications, clinic procedures when indicated, and smart routine adjustments to minimize irritation while promoting healthier skin.

What to Expect During Treatment

Your plan may include topical retinoids for comedone control, benzoyl peroxide for antibacterial action, and azelaic acid or salicylic acid for texture refinement. For inflammatory or resistant acne, oral antibiotics, hormonal modulation, or isotretinoin may be considered under medical supervision. Procedures such as chemical peels, light-based therapies, or comedone extraction can complement medical care for faster clarity and smoother texture.

We emphasize realistic timelines: acne improves gradually over weeks as the skin renews. Consistency and follow-up are critical. Our team provides clear instructions on application, moisturization strategies, sun protection, and lifestyle guidance. For patients with pigmentation or post-inflammatory marks, adjuvant care can even skin tone safely.

Acne Scars and Skin Texture

When scarring is present, Skin 360 offers treatments such as microneedling, laser resurfacing, subcision, and targeted chemical reconstruction techniques. Scar care is sequenced carefully after active acne is controlled. Our objective is meaningful, natural-looking improvements while preserving skin integrity.
