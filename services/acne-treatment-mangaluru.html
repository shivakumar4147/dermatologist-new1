Understanding Acne: Causes and Types

Acne is a complex skin condition that affects millions of people. It's not just a teenage problem; adult acne is also very common. Our skin specialist in Mangaluru identifies the following key factors that contribute to breakouts:

  • Sebum Production: Excess oil can clog pores and create an environment for bacteria.
  • Clogged Pores: Dead skin cells that aren't shed properly can trap oil and bacteria.
  • Bacterial Growth: Cutibacterium acnes can thrive in clogged pores, causing inflammation.
  • Inflammation: The body's immune response to the bacteria leads to red, painful bumps.
  • Hormonal Fluctuations: Changes during puberty, menstruation, or stress can trigger breakouts.

Acne presents in several forms—whiteheads, blackheads, papules, pustules, nodules, and cysts. Each type requires a different approach to treatment to ensure effective results and minimize the risk of scarring.

When to See a Dermatologist for Acne?

While minor breakouts can sometimes be managed at home, many situations require professional help from a dermatologist in Mangaluru:

  • Persistent Breakouts: If over-the-counter products haven't worked after 4-6 weeks.
  • Painful or Deep Cysts: These are more likely to cause permanent scarring and need medical intervention.
  • Sudden Onset: If acne appears suddenly in adulthood or after starting a new medication.
  • Risk of Scarring: If you notice dark spots or pitted scars forming after pimples heal.
  • Emotional Impact: If skin concerns are affecting your self-confidence or social life.

Personalized Treatment Options

Your plan may include topical retinoids for comedone control, benzoyl peroxide for antibacterial action, and azelaic acid or salicylic acid for texture refinement. For inflammatory or resistant acne, oral antibiotics, hormonal modulation, or isotretinoin may be considered under medical supervision.

In addition to medication, we offer advanced clinical procedures at our skin clinic in Mangaluru:

  • Chemical Peels: Salicylic or mandelic acid peels to unclog pores and reduce oiliness.
  • Comedone Extraction: Professional removal of blackheads and whiteheads to prevent inflammation.
  • Light and Laser Therapy: To target acne-causing bacteria and reduce redness.
  • Intralesional Injections: Quick relief for deep, painful cystic acne.

Benefits of Professional Acne Care

Consulting the best dermatologist in Mangaluru offers several advantages over self-treatment:

  • Accurate Diagnosis: Differentiating acne from other conditions like rosacea or perioral dermatitis.
  • Prevention of Scarring: Early and effective treatment is the best way to avoid permanent marks.
  • Faster Results: Prescription-strength treatments work more effectively than OTC options.
  • Comprehensive Guidance: Advice on skincare routines, diet, and lifestyle to maintain clear skin.
