Comprehensive Dermatology Care

Skin 360 provides structured, evidence-based treatment plans for common and complex skin concerns. We begin with a detailed consultation that considers your medical history, current symptoms, lifestyle, routines, and prior treatments. Each plan is tailored to your skin type and sensitivity to maximize efficacy while preserving barrier health.

Acne Treatment

Acne care targets comedones, inflammation, and post-acne marks through a staged approach. Topicals like retinoids, benzoyl peroxide, and azelaic acid are balanced with moisturization and sun protection. Oral therapies are introduced when necessary. Clinic procedures such as chemical peels and light therapies can accelerate clarity. For scar management, options include microneedling, subcision, and resurfacing planned after active acne control. Learn more about acne treatment.

Pigmentation Treatment

Uneven skin tone may arise from post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, melasma, sun exposure, or hormonal influences. Our dermatologist designs a regimen with tyrosinase inhibitors, exfoliants, antioxidants, and strict photoprotection to gradually brighten skin. In-clinic treatments may include gentle chemical peels or laser protocols selected for your skin type. Maintenance plans reduce recurrence and support long-term clarity. Explore pigmentation treatment.

Skin Allergy Treatment

Allergic and irritant dermatitis requires precise identification of triggers and barrier repair strategies. We guide appropriate topical anti-inflammatory care, avoidance of sensitizers, and restoration routines. Patch testing may be considered based on history. Education on product selection and routine discipline is central to preventing flares. Read about allergy treatment.

Skin Infection Treatment

Bacterial, viral, and fungal infections are assessed clinically and treated with targeted medications. We emphasize correct diagnosis, appropriate antimicrobial stewardship, and supportive care to restore skin health. Preventive advice helps reduce recurrences. View infection care.

Your Treatment Journey

Expect clear instructions, realistic timelines, and supportive follow-ups. We prioritize gentle routines, evidence-based medications, and stepwise clinic procedures when indicated. Our aim is healthy, comfortable skin with dependable results you can trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see results? Initial improvements often appear within 4–6 weeks for acne and pigmentation, with continued gains over subsequent months. Procedures may accelerate visible change, but consistency with daily care matters most.

Will treatment irritate my skin? Plans are designed around tolerance. We titrate actives, support the barrier with moisturization, and adjust frequency to maintain comfort while achieving clinical goals.

Do I need to change my routine? We simplify and refine routines to avoid sensitizers and redundancy. Guidance covers cleansers, moisturizers, sunscreen, and targeted actives appropriate to your skin type.

Are results permanent? Skin health is dynamic. Maintenance strategies and sun protection help preserve results. For pigmentation, photoprotection is essential; for acne, long-term control is typical with appropriate care.

Is treatment safe for my skin tone? Yes. We select topical and procedural options that respect your skin’s physiology, avoiding overly aggressive protocols. Safety and trust are paramount at Skin 360.
